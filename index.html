About the Artist

BIO

Summer is an Indian music producer making mainly Future bass, trap and related genres. Since very young age summer had interest in Music and Graphic designing. He is still continuing the both of his passion…

Starting off

When Summer was 14 years old, once listening to music, A thought came in mind that how music is made. And that curiosity led him to install and computer software called Fl studio. Summer started making beats on it watching tutorials online..

Electronic Music

As summer got littler experienced in making beats he started making electronic music following online video tutorials, posts and article which were available for free.
